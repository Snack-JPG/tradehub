## Design Direction
- **Colours:** Use the logo colours (check the .webp) + dark/professional tones. Electricians = think navy, yellow/amber accents (electrical theme), white space
- **Feel:** Professional but approachable. Not corporate. This is a local tradesperson, not a FTSE 100 company
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif. Nothing fancy
- **Mobile:** 70%+ of visitors will be on phones. Hero CTA must be thumb-reachable. Click-to-call everywhere
- **Speed:** Must score 90+ on Lighthouse. No bloat

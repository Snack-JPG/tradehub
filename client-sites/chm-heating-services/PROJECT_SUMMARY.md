## Design Philosophy

### Aesthetic Direction: "Editorial Warmth"

The design blends editorial magazine sophistication with heating industry trust signals:

- **Color Palette**: Warm rust/amber tones (#f15b0b) paired with deep navy (#1a2332)
- **Typography**: Libre Baskerville (display) + Inter (body) for refined professionalism
- **Visual Language**: Radiator-line patterns, warmth gradients, generous whitespace
- **Tone**: Professional but approachable - trustworthy craftsman, not corporate giant

### Key Design Decisions

1. **Warm, Not Hot**: Orange/rust evokes heat without being aggressive
2. **Editorial Hierarchy**: Large display typography creates magazine-like elegance
3. **Trust Through Space**: Generous whitespace = confidence and clarity
4. **Social Proof Forward**: 5‚òÖ rating visible immediately and throughout
5. **Emergency Focus**: Pulsing emergency bar creates urgency without panic

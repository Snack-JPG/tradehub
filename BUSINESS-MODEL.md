# TradeHub — Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Website Builds (One-Off)
| Tier | Price | What They Get |
|------|-------|---------------|
| **Starter** | £100–200 | Single-page site, mobile-responsive, basic SEO, Google Business Profile setup |
| **Professional** | £400–500 | Multi-page site, full SEO, contact forms, reviews integration, directory listing |
| **Premium** | £750–1,000 | Everything above + blog setup, Google Ads landing pages, advanced schema, content writing |

### 2. Hosting & Maintenance — £30/mo
- Vercel/Netlify hosting
- SSL, domain management
- Monthly updates, uptime monitoring
- Directory listing included

### 3. Directory Listings — £10–20/mo
For tradespeople who don't want a full website but want to appear in the directory.
- Profile page with photo, services, reviews, contact info
- Backlink if they have an existing site
- Appears in category + location pages

### 4. Featured Listings — £50/mo
- Top of category/location pages
- Highlighted card with "Featured" badge
- Priority in search results
- Larger profile card on homepage

### 5. Lead Generation — £30–50/lead
- Contact form submissions routed to relevant tradesperson
- Phone call tracking (future)
- Pay-per-lead model for high-value trades (kitchens, bathrooms, extensions)

### 6. Google Ads Management — £150–300/mo
- Campaign setup and management
- Landing page optimisation
- Monthly reporting
- Best for trades with high job values (£1k+)

## Growth Flywheel

```
Build website for tradesperson
        ↓
List them on TradeHub directory
        ↓
Directory pages rank for "[trade] [location]"
        ↓
Homeowners find directory → contact tradesperson
        ↓
Tradesperson gets leads → sees value → refers others
        ↓
New tradespeople sign up → more listings → more pages → better rankings
        ↓
Cycle accelerates
```

**Key insight:** Every new client makes the directory stronger, which makes it easier to sell to the next client.

## Referral Program

**Refer 1 tradesperson → Free hosting for 12 months (£360 value)**

- Simple to explain on a call
- Tradespeople know other tradespeople (plumber knows a sparky, sparky knows a builder)
- Cost to us: £0–20/mo hosting cost vs £360 perceived value
- Creates network effects in local trade communities

## Competitive Advantage

### vs Checkatrade (£500–1,000+/year, pay-per-lead)
- We're cheaper with more value (actual website + listing)
- No extortionate lead fees
- Client owns their website — no lock-in
- We build their online presence, not just a listing

### vs Yell.com (outdated, expensive, poor ROI)
- Modern tech, actually ranks on Google
- Transparent pricing
- Sites that look good and convert

### vs Local Agencies (£2,000–5,000 for a website)
- 10x cheaper for equivalent quality
- AI-powered delivery = faster turnaround
- Ongoing relationship, not just a one-off build

### vs DIY (Wix, Squarespace)
- Done-for-them — tradespeople are busy
- SEO baked in from day one (schema, llms.txt, backlinks)
- Directory backlink + listing included
- Professional result without the learning curve

### Our Edge: AI-Powered SEO
- **llms.txt** on every site — optimised for AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI)
- **Full JSON-LD schema** — LocalBusiness, Service, Review markup
- **Automated sitemap + meta** — zero manual SEO work
- **Speed** — sites built in hours, not weeks

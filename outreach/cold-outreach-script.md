## ðŸ”‘ Key Principles

### DO:
- **Build the site before you reach out.** The link is the closer.
- **Lead with their reviews.** Everyone loves hearing they're good at their job.
- **Be casual and fast.** This isn't a boardroom pitch, it's a chat.
- **Send the link within 30 seconds.** Don't describe it, show it.
- **Sound like you're doing them a favour** (because you are).

### DON'T:
- Don't say "would you be interested in..." â€” you already built it, it's done.
- Don't oversell TradeHub. Keep focus on THEM.
- Don't apologise for calling. You're bringing good news.
- Don't mention competitors (Checkatrade, MyBuilder etc) unless they do.
- Don't ask permission to send the link. Just send it.

## After They Say Yes

1. Confirm their details are correct (phone, services, areas)
2. Ask if they have any photos of their work they'd like on there
3. Tell them you'll check in in a week to see how it's going
4. Add them to the CRM / tracking sheet

---

## Volume Target

Aim for **5 outreach messages per session.** Even a 30% conversion rate = ~15 listings from 50 attempts. Most will say yes because there's literally no downside for them.
